# Apex Capital VC Methodology

Apex Capital uses a strict, multi-gated deterministic logic engine enhanced by AI.

1. **Deterministic First, AI Second:** Hard rules control decisions. A startup with low evidence scores or critical risks cannot be recommended for investment, regardless of the AI's enthusiasm.
2. **Public vs. Private:** For public benchmarks (e.g. Zepto, Mistral), we strictly separate public facts from private assumptions. The AI cannot hallucinate private diligence data.
3. **Power-Law Scoring:** Deals are evaluated based on their ability to return the fund, not just generate a safe outcome.
4. **Fund Fit:** We calculate exact ownership targets, dilution, and reserve allocation based on a standard $12M (₹100Cr) early-stage fund model.

## Agentic Methodology
Apex Capital uses LLMs as specialist analysts, not final decision makers. Each agent works from structured source evidence, and deterministic gates still control recommendation caps. The Red Team agent acts as a mandatory skeptic, adjusting the final confidence score downwards when evidence is purely media-reported or when private metrics are missing.
