## 3-Minute Version

**[Screen: Landing Page]**
"Apex Capital is not just a startup analyzer or memo generator. It is an investment workflow system that moves a company from founder narrative to evidence-backed judgment."

**[Screen: Presentation]**
"The backend is modular: analysis engine, research engine, deck engine, diligence engine, conversation engine, decision engine, fund engine, and AI provider router. It runs in mock mode for demos and can later route tasks to Gemini, Claude, or OpenAI."

**[Screen: Command Center]**
"The Command Center gives partners a single operational dashboard to see fund health, active deals, and IC readiness at a glance."

**[Screen: Compare Deals]**
"We can stack-rank our active pipeline—for instance, comparing NeuralDesk against VetPulse AI—to see which deal has the strongest thesis and fewest evidence gaps."

**[Screen: NeuralDesk Deal Room]**
"Diving into NeuralDesk, the deal room acts as our source of truth. We immediately see the core thesis and risks."

**[Screen: Conversation Intelligence]**
"But we don't just trust the pitch deck. Our Conversation Intelligence engine parses multi-round founder-investor transcripts. It flags evasive answers, tracks open follow-ups, and highlights contradiction risk between what the founder claims and what the market shows."

**[Screen: Diligence Command Center]**
"Any missing evidence or contradiction automatically spawns a Diligence action item. It even generates the exact reference call scripts we need to verify the claims."

**[Screen: Memo]**
"The key design principle is that a high Apex Score does not automatically mean Invest. Evidence quality, founder conversations, unresolved diligence, fund fit, and IC readiness can all cap or downgrade the recommendation. The final Investment Memo guarantees a rigorous, data-backed decision."
